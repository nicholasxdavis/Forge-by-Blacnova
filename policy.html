Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 15, 2025

1. Data Collection

Forge collects minimal data to operate our services effectively:

  • Account Information: Email and basic profile details for account creation
  • Tool Usage: Anonymous usage metrics to improve our tools (whiteboard, calendar, file compressor, etc.)
  • Cookies: Essential cookies for functionality and optional analytics cookies

2. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data across all tools:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive tools (encryption, notes, task manager)
  • Regular security audits of all systems
  • Data minimization principles applied throughout

3. Advertising

Beginning in 2025, Forge may display non-intrusive ads to support development:

  • Ads will be clearly marked and separated from content
  • No personalized/targeted ads will use your private data
  • Premium plans will remain ad-free

4. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access, edit, or delete your account information
  • Export your data from any tool (spreadsheets, notes, etc.)
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection

Terms of Service

1. Acceptable Use

You agree not to misuse Forge tools including but not limited to:

  • Reverse engineering or copying any functionality
  • Using tools for illegal activities (encryption, QR codes, etc.)
  • Automated scraping of our services

2. Tool-Specific Terms

  • Extensions: Browser extensions respect your privacy but require necessary permissions

3. Liability

Forge provides tools "as-is" without warranties:

4. Changes

  • Continued use constitutes acceptance
  • Archive of past policies available on request

Cookies Policy

How we use cookies and similar technologies

1. What We Use Cookies For

Forge uses cookies and similar tracking technologies across all tools to:

  • Maintain secure user sessions (especially for tools like encryption and task manager)
  • Remember preferences in tools like the designer and whiteboard
  • Gather analytics to improve performance (spreadsheets, data visualization)
  • Support future advertising implementation (planned for 2025)

2. Types of Cookies

Category Purpose Examples
Essential Core functionality Login sessions, tool preferences
Performance Analytics Usage metrics for file compressor, calendar
Functional Enhanced features Whiteboard settings, QR code history
Advertising* Future ad support Non-personalized content delivery

*Advertising cookies will be implemented with clear opt-out options

3. Extension-Specific Tracking

Our browser extensions (Tasks, Calendar, Color Picker, etc.) use minimal local storage:

  • Only stores data needed for functionality
  • No cross-site tracking
  • Clear permission requirements during installation
