Who We Are

Built for Makers, by Makers Who Get It

Let's be real - most productivity tools suck. They're either too bloated or missing key features. That's why we created Forge. We're a team of creators who actually use our own products daily. No corporate fluff, just tools that work.

Our Mission

We build tools that actually help you work, not distract you. No unnecessary features. No learning curves that require a PhD. Just clean, focused apps that disappear into your workflow until you need them.

How We Work

Transparency isn't just a buzzword for us. We build in public, share our wins and fails, and actually listen to feedback. Most of our best features came from user suggestions. Join our Discord and you'll see - we're always there.

What We've Built

Forge started as a simple toolkit and grew into a full suite because that's what we needed. Whiteboards that don't lag. A task manager that doesn't get in your way. File tools that just work. Every feature exists because we got tired of crappy alternatives.

Browser Power-Ups

Our extensions are like having a Swiss Army knife in your browser. Notes that sync everywhere. A color picker that remembers your palettes. QR generator that just works. We keep adding more because honestly, why should simple tasks be complicated?

Join the Crew

This isn't just our product - it's yours too. Thousands of creators shape Forge daily through feedback and ideas. Our community gets early access, voting power on new features, and real conversations with our team. No support bots here.
